Learning Modules

💡 Science-backed: This app uses spaced repetition (items repeat at expanding intervals: 1→3→7→21 days) and active recall (you retrieve answers rather than re-read). Research shows 200–400% better retention than passive study. Aim for 30 min/day for 4–6 months for N5.

6-Month Study Plan

Based on Genki I framework + N5 syllabus · ~30 min/day

Kana Mastery

Hiragana (46) + Katakana (46) — foundation of all reading

Technique: Learn shape + sound together, use mnemonics. Once you know hiragana, you can read Japanese. Target: master both scripts in 2 weeks.

Vocabulary · 800 Words

Organized by category · click words to track progress

N5 requires ~800 words. Learn in context-rich categories. High-frequency verbs first, then adjectives, then nouns. Click a word to mark it as reviewed (+XP).

Grammar · 60 Patterns

Every N5 pattern with examples

Strategy: Master particles first (they're in every sentence). Then verb forms (ます, て, た, ない). Click each pattern to expand with example sentences.

Kanji · 103 Characters

N5 kanji with readings, meaning & vocabulary context

Learn in context: Don't memorize isolated characters. Learn 日 through 日本 (Japan) and 今日 (today). Aim for 3–5 new kanji per day.

Ask Sensei

Your AI Japanese tutor — powered by Claude

Use this to: Ask "why" questions about grammar, get sentences corrected, request vocabulary by topic, practice sentences, or clarify anything confusing.

Conversation Tutor

Speak or type in real scenarios — mic enabled · corrections inline

How to use: Pick a scenario, then tap the 🎤 mic button and speak in Japanese (or type). Yuki replies in Japanese with romaji + English, corrects mistakes naturally, and speaks the response aloud. Best in Chrome or Edge.
